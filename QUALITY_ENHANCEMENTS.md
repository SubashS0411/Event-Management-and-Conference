- Dropdown menus work on hover (desktop) and tap (mobile)
- Home 1 / Home 2 navigation implemented consistently
- Mobile-friendly spacing and touch targets

#### Newsletter Forms
- Form inputs stack on mobile: `flex-col`

- Full-width buttons on mobile, inline on desktop
